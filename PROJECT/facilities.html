AT The Moment, we have two branches

RElish Bangalore

Opened in 2017,this is our Main Branch.As it's is common knowledge that how much young IT professionals crave for physical activity, we(IT professionals ourselves)decided to setup a sports centre where anybody from Youth to the Old can engage in physical activity amidst the pollution and industrialization that has been destroying the open grounds we once used to play. also with state of the art Facilities,we also provide coaching to young kids in cricket,football,table tennis for their fitness as well. we always try to keep our Facilities as organised and clean as possible.We are also working on providing water sports in the future as well.

RElish Vizag

Although opened in 2021,this our Fastest growing branch. Located along the beautiful beach road in the Jewel of East Coast in Vizag,it makes sense to keep our water sports haedquarters here. By the beautiful,warm sand of the beach,We regularly hold training sessions,competitions. the water sports offered are surfing,sailing,water skiing etc,.We also rent facilities for cricket,football and tennis.we are working on providing coaching for cricket,football and tennis.
